French Omelette

Home

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter

Filling

  • 250g Fresh mushrooms
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1tbsp Butter
  • Finely chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice the mushrooms. Peel and finely chop the garlic.
  2. In a frying pan, sauté the mushrooms and garlic in the butter for about five minutes. Stir in the parsley, salt and pepper. Keep the filling warm.
  3. Lightly whisk together the eggs, milk and salt.
  4. Heat the butter until golden brown in a frying pan. Fry the omelette batter on a medium-low heat in the frying pan for approx. four minutes.
  5. Gently stir the egg mixture a few times during the cooking process.
  6. When the egg has fully set but is still slightly creamy on the surface, add the filling in the middle of the omelette.
  7. Holding the pan’s handle, gently start rolling the omelette into a cylindrical shape with your spatula. When you have a few centimetres left, shake the omelette to the edge of the pan and gently flip it onto your plate, seam-side down.
